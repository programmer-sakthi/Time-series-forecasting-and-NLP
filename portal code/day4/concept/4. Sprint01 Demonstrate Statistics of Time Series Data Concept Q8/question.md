Problem Statement

After evaluating multiple smoothing approaches, a final exponential smoothing model is applied to best represent the underlying behavior of electricity demand. 

The final exponential smoothing result provides a refined version of the original data that closely follows long-term trends and recurring seasonal movements while minimizing random fluctuations.

Identify reductions in noise while retaining key patterns. 
Evaluate trend preservation

Assess seasonal pattern retention

Analyze smoothing effectiveness

Assess the balance between smoothness and responsiveness. 
Support forecasting and decision-making

Facilitate improved energy demand planning and policy decisions.
